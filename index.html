ABOUT US


I'm Elysia Karis E. Gulbin, an Information Technology (IT) student and has interest in music and art. Being an IT student isn’t just about learning how to code, it’s about understanding the creativity that underlies technology. I’m particularly interested in how coding can play a central role in bringing these creative visions to life. Instead of focusing on my weaknesses, I try to remember that learning is a process, not a final goal. Everyone has things they’re good at and things they struggle with, and not being perfect doesn’t mean I’m not a good student. I’ve learned that asking for help and recognizing my small improvements is important for building confidence. As a student, life is full of challenges, but those challenges are also what shape us and help us grow. From playing the piano to strumming the guitar, painting ,drawing, music and arts has given me a way to express emotions and ideas that helps comfort myself I cannot convey.
